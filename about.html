Who am I?

Hi, my name is Sharam Khan, and I am an 18-year-old student currently specializing in Cybersecurity. This field has captivated me due to its essential role in safeguarding the digital landscape. Ever since I was young, I have been deeply passionate about technology and innovation, which naturally led me to explore areas like problem-solving, system protection, and ethical hacking. I am an individual who thrives on challenges and is motivated by the opportunity to make a meaningful difference. My goal is to continue growing as a cybersecurity professional, with a strong focus on both technical skills and the ethical principles required to protect individuals, organizations, and critical infrastructures in an increasingly connected world.



What do I do?

As a student of Cybersecurity, I am actively engaged in a variety of projects and initiatives that hone my skills and prepare me for real-world challenges. I focus on penetration testing, analyzing vulnerabilities, and working with tools to strengthen network security. Additionally, I have been learning about encryption techniques, firewall configurations, and how to detect and respond to cyber threats effectively.

Some of the hands-on projects I have worked on include simulated network intrusions, where I identify and mitigate potential security risks, and building secure system environments to understand the importance of layered security protocols. My work often involves staying updated with the latest cybersecurity trends, experimenting with cutting-edge tools, and conducting thorough research to improve digital defense mechanisms. I am passionate about contributing to a safer digital ecosystem and constantly seek opportunities to apply my knowledge in innovative ways.

My Skills and Values

I am driven by a strong desire to make a meaningful impact in the field of Cybersecurity. Here are some highlights of my skills and what I stand for:

  • Technical Expertise: Knowledge in penetration testing, firewall setup, and intrusion detection systems.
