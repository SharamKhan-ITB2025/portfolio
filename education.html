Advanced Degree in ASIX (Systems Integration in Networks) - ITB Barcelona

I completed the "Advanced Degree" in ASIX (Systems Integration in Networks) at ITB Barcelona, specializing in Cybersecurity. This program provided me with an in-depth understanding of advanced IT systems, with a particular focus on securing systems against potential threats. During the course, I studied key topics such as network security, ethical hacking, cryptography, and the implementation of security protocols like firewalls and intrusion detection systems.

Through hands-on labs and real-world case studies, I developed strong skills in identifying vulnerabilities, preventing cyberattacks, and protecting sensitive data. This program prepared me to work as a cybersecurity professional, a critical role in the tech industry.

Intermediate Degree in SMX (Microcomputer Systems and Networks) - ITB Barcelona

Before specializing in cybersecurity, I completed the "Intermediate Degree" in SMX (Microcomputer Systems and Networks) at ITB Barcelona. In this program, I gained a comprehensive understanding of various aspects of IT, including network management, hardware maintenance, and system configurations. Throughout the course, I learned how to manage and maintain different types of operating systems, configure networks, and troubleshoot common IT issues.

This training provided me with essential practical experience in setting up, maintaining, and troubleshooting computer networks, hardware components, and peripheral devices. These skills enabled me to take my first steps into the tech world, preparing me for technical roles in IT support and system management.
