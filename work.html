Penetration Testing

This project simulates a cyber attack designed to identify vulnerabilities in systems and networks, with the goal of conducting a thorough analysis to discover security flaws that could be exploited by malicious attackers. Through various testing techniques and evaluation methods, weaknesses in configurations, communication protocols, and applications are examined, all with the intent of preventing potential exploitation and protecting an organization's digital assets from external threats. This approach not only enhances security but also strengthens the ability to respond to real-world attacks, ensuring a more secure and robust environment.

Intrusion Detection System

This project involves the implementation of an Intrusion Detection System (IDS), which is designed to monitor network traffic and detect any suspicious activities or potential threats that could compromise the security of a network. By analyzing network patterns, identifying anomalies, and generating alerts in real-time, the IDS plays a critical role in enhancing the security posture of an organization. This system helps to promptly identify and respond to any intrusions or attacks, providing an additional layer of defense to prevent unauthorized access, data breaches, or other forms of cyber threats that could jeopardize the integrity of the network.
